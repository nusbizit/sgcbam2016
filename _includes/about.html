About

Singapore GeoSpatial Challange Business Analytics Mania (SGCBAM) is a nationwide competition which aims to introduce the use of Data Analytics tools for business organisations to students, giving them an opportunity to learn business analytics tools to move Singapore towards becoming a Smart Nation.

The Challenge

On the day of the competition, teams will be given a set of raw data, and will learn to use SAS data analytics software to gain innovative business insights and formulate proposals. The problem statement for the challenge will be released on the competition day itself.

Prizes

All participants in the challenge will receive certificates of participation. Outstanding teams also have an opportunity to win attractive prizes!
